A black box for your car.

Tamper-evident incident evidence, assembled and signed entirely on your phone.

Tessera records the road continuously, detects incidents, and seals each one into a cryptographically signed dossier — video, motion, and GPS — that never leaves your device. No account. No cloud. No subscription, ever.

Every incident is sealed with a hardware-backed signature and a C2PA content credential — verifiable by anyone, with no trust in us required.

What you get

  • Continuous loop recording with automatic storage management.
  • Automatic incident detection, plus one-tap and “Hey Siri” marking.
  • Hardware-backed signatures and C2PA content credentials on every dossier.
  • An on-device AI narrative that only states what the signed telemetry shows.
  • Court-ready export: video, telemetry, signature, and a one-page PDF report.

What you don’t get

  • A server. There isn’t one — the app works in airplane mode.
  • An account, a login, or any data collection. Ever.
  • A subscription.
